Create one cute educational illustration for the answer side of an Anki card, for a Chinese middle school student (grade 7).

Aspect ratio: 4:3 study card.
Language on image: Simplified Chinese, short labels only.
Subject and level: History, 7th grade second semester textbook level.

Key concept to visualize:
1689 年，中俄签订《尼布楚条约》，从法律上肯定了黑龙江和乌苏里江流域包括库页岛在内的广大地区都是中国领土。

Composition:
Two cute chibi characters from different sides shaking hands (or holding a scroll together), representing the peace agreement. A happy dove or peace symbol above them. On the side, a small balance scale shows 'peace + trade' outweighing the 'tribute' payment, addressing the misconception that it was completely negative.

Required Chinese labels, short and friendly:
1. 1689 年
2. 中俄签订《尼布楚条约》
3. 从法律上肯定了黑龙江和乌苏里江流域包括库页岛在内的广大地区都是中国领土。

Warning/Misconception:
不要把《尼布楚条约》写成不平等条约。教材中它是平等协商签订的边界条约。

Visual style:
Cute educational illustration for middle schoolers. Warm cream background with soft pastel accents. Rounded, soft shapes with gentle outlines. Friendly chibi-style characters (big head, small body, simple cute faces) demonstrating the concept. Warm orange/yellow for highlights. Soft blue for maps/secondary info. Coral/pink for emphasis. Red only for warning boxes. Rounded speech bubbles and signposts. Large, legible text. Generous white space. NO dark backgrounds, NO sharp geometric shapes, NO realistic portraits.

Constraints:
Do NOT include the question text in the image — this is the answer side of an Anki card, the student has already seen the question. Do not add long paragraphs. Do not invent facts, dates, or place names. Do not use tiny text. Do not make a PPT slide or commercial poster. Every cute element must serve the learning goal.
